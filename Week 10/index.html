Some Games I've Played Recently

I got Baulder’s Gate 3 pretty quick after it came out at the behest of my sister. I really enjoy playing it as I’m a big DnD fan. I often play with my sister and a few other folks. I want to play more by myself but I tend to enjoy playing with other people more. The story is pretty fun and engaging. I have a couple of characters and I would like to be able to play through all the classes at some point.
Hades is a wonderful game for any fan of Rouge-lites. Supergiant brings their A game with the art and music as usual. It has such a fun gameplay loop that even folks that usually aren’t Rouge-lite fans, like myself, are able to enjoy the game and story. Zagerus is a fun protagonist to play. The ability to develop his relationships with fellow gods and those of the underworld is a great way to create the game’s narrative. It also makes me wish for a Supergiant dating sim.
Splatoon has been the first shooter type game I have ever played and really enjoy. It’s colorful charm and characters really endears it to me. I’ve never been a fan of realistic or military shooters so a game that is bright and fun as Splatoon is what really brought me to the genre. I play with friends or even by myself as a way to let out some steam after doing a lot of homework. Salmon Run is one of my favorite game modes as it's more collaborative in nature then the other modes.
Stardew Valley is the most recent game I’ve picked up. Farming sims are a genre that I figured I would enjoy and Stardew is a very popular and accessible game. I’ve had it for a while, just never had the time to play it. I’m still pretty early on in the game so there’s a bunch I haven’t experienced yet but I’m excited.
